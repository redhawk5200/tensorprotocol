    Introduction

    Through our innovative bot, users can unleash their creativity by conceptualizing and constructing custom rooms measuring 20’ x 20’. Whether it's a virtual office, a digital hangout spot, or a vibrant art gallery, the possibilities are endless. Each user has the autonomy to curate and personalize their virtual space to reflect their unique interests and preferences.

    Integrate up to 20 chats

    One of the most exciting features of MetaTG Ai is its ability to integrate up to 20 telegram groups & chats within a single virtual environment — unleashing the potential for dynamic and interactive group discussions within a captivating spatial setting. Imagine hosting engaging conversations, brainstorming sessions, or even social gatherings, all within the visually stunning and interactive metaverse you've created.
